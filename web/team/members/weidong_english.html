Research Interests

I am interested about the multiple view geometry in computer vision and the graph theory. Currently, I am studying about 3D reconstruction with line features based on graph theories and GPU acceleration, some of them are:
• 3D reconstruction with line and point features;
• Joint scene plane theory and graph matching for line segment matching;
• Line segment extraction.
